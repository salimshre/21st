## 🔁 Step Definitions

### ✅ Step 1 — Focused Learning Block
- **30 min** focused work → **5 min** break
- Break activity: **meditate** OR do **one set** of physical workout
- Repeat the cycle for the full block duration

### 🍽️ Step 2 — Meal Block
- Start a **countdown timer** (6–30 min) for dinner/meal preparation
- Eat and rest → pack steel lunchbox for next day
- **15 min rest** after the meal
- Total block: **1 hour**

> **⚠️ Step 2 Override Rule:** If food is not yet cooked when the block starts, take a longer break to help with cooking. After returning, **re-read this schedule** to mentally refocus — this activity helps with **deep focus and learning retention**.
